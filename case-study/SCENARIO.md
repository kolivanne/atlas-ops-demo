# Scenario: Scaling Atlassian in a Gaming Company

A fast-growing company (~150 employees) faces increasing complexity in its tooling stack.

## Problems

- 30+ Jira workflows with inconsistent logic
- 1000+ custom fields, many unused
- Cluttered Confluence spaces
- No structured AI governance

## Impact

- Slower onboarding
- Confusing workflows
- Reduced productivity
- Increased compliance risk

## Goal

- Reduce complexity by 50%
- Standardize workflows
- Clean up knowledge base
- Introduce safe AI usage
