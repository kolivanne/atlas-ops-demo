# Scenario: Scaling Atlassian in a Fast-Growing Company

## Context
A gaming company with ~150 employees experiences rapid growth, resulting in increased complexity across its Atlassian tooling stack.  

**Challenge:** Without governance, onboarding, productivity, and compliance are impacted.

---

## Problems

| Area | Current State | Impact | KPI / Target |
|------|---------------|--------|--------------|
| Jira Workflows | 30+ workflows, inconsistent logic | Confusing for teams, slower onboarding | Reduce to 15 standardized workflows |
| Custom Fields | 1000+ fields, many unused | Data clutter, slow issue creation | Reduce by 50%, remove unused fields |
| Confluence Spaces | Cluttered, outdated content | Hard to find knowledge, low trust | Archive stale pages, standardize templates |
| AI Governance | No structured framework | Compliance risk, inconsistent usage | Implement AI policy and automated request workflow |

---

## Goals
- Reduce Jira and Confluence complexity by 50%  
- Standardize Jira workflows and issue types  
- Clean up Confluence knowledge base with ownership and templates  
- Introduce safe and compliant AI usage  
- Implement measurable KPIs and dashboards for monitoring

---

## Proposed Approach
1. **Assessment & Audit**
   - Inventory all workflows, fields, spaces, and AI usage
   - Identify redundancies, inconsistencies, and risks
2. **Prioritization**
   - Target high-impact areas first (critical workflows, frequently used fields, core Confluence spaces)
3. **Standardization & Cleanup**
   - Consolidate workflows and issue types
   - Archive or restructure outdated Confluence content
   - Apply standard templates and naming conventions
4. **Governance & Automation**
   - Define ownership for workflows, fields, and spaces
   - Automate notifications for stale tickets, fields, and pages
   - Implement AI request workflow with compliance checks
5. **Monitoring & KPIs**
   - Dashboards tracking workflow usage, SLA adherence, stale content, AI request compliance
   - Quarterly audits to ensure continuous improvement

---

## Expected Outcomes
- Reduced complexity and faster onboarding  
- Consistent workflows and issue structures  
- Improved knowledge accessibility and trust in documentation  
- Safe and compliant AI usage across teams  
- Metrics-driven decision making for tooling improvements
