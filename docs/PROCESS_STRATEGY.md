# IT Process Optimization Strategy

This document outlines the framework for managing IT workflows, tools, and infrastructure in a high-growth environment. The focus is on reducing technical debt, improving process clarity, and enabling teams to operate efficiently.

## 1. Governance Decisions
- IT retains control over workflow creation and modification.
- Teams can configure boards, filters, and dashboards.
- New custom fields require IT approval via a formal request process.

**Tradeoffs:**
- Reduced flexibility for teams
- Increased clarity and maintainability for IT and operations

## 2. Stakeholder Alignment
- **Visualization:** Complex workflows are represented using flow diagrams for non-technical stakeholders.
- **Feedback Loops:** Quarterly reviews of processes, tools, and licenses to optimize costs and remove unused assets.
- **Alignment Goals:**
  - Ensure Engineering and Business Operations have a shared understanding of priorities
  - Maintain clear communication about process changes

## 3. Automation Roadmap
Automation focuses on areas where manual work creates bottlenecks, with staged implementation:

1. **Triage Automation**
   - Requests and issues are automatically tagged and assigned
   - Reduces manual triage time and human error

2. **Validation Automation**
   - Selected requests undergo automated checks (e.g., security or compliance validations)
   - Ensures consistent policy enforcement without slowing teams

3. **Archiving & Lifecycle Management**
   - Programmatic cleanup of legacy documentation and inactive workflows
   - Keeps knowledge base accurate and reduces clutter

**Metrics to Monitor:**
- Time saved through automation
- % of legacy items archived
- Stakeholder satisfaction with process clarity

## 4. Audit & Review
- Governance and process audits occur quarterly
- Outcomes inform next iteration of process improvements
- Focus on identifying bottlenecks and optimizing team velocity

## 5. Communication & Training
- Governance rules and processes are documented in a central repository
- Training sessions for teams on new workflows, templates, and automation best practices
- Feedback is continuously collected to refine processes

**Note:** This document demonstrates the strategic approach and priorities for IT process optimization while leaving detailed implementation specifics internal.
